My name is Ryan Tilque. I was born and raised in Western Washington. I attended Bothell High School before moving to Spokane where I attended Spokane Falls Community College. There, I received an Associates of Business degree. I then transferred to Eastern Washington University in the fall of 2014. At EWU I earned a Bachelor of Arts in Business Administration with an emphasis in Marketing accompanied with a minor in Visual Communication Design. Currently residing in Western Washington again, with a career at Costco Wholesale. In my free time I like to go snowboarding in the winter while camping and hiking in the summer.
